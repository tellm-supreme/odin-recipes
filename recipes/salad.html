Caesar Salad

This classic Caesar salad is a refreshing and healthy choice for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/2 cup of Caesar dressing
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of croutons
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the lettuce with the Caesar dressing until evenly coated.
  3. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and croutons, and toss again.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve the salad with lemon wedges on the side for extra flavor.
